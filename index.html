About Me

Hi!

My name is Ihor Spyrydonov and I am Front-End developer.

Since 2014 I have been actively interested in Front-End. My layout is valid, adaptive, accessible and cross-browser.

I love working with smooth transitions and CSS animations. I prefer vanilla JavaScript but I'm ready to work with any frameworks.

As a developer, I consider myself a fan of what I do, with a "sense of beauty". I fulfill my tasks responsibly. I pay special attention to optimization.

I am deeply convinced that creating a high-quality website is possible only under the following conditions:

  • logical HTML markup;
  • convenient and accessible interface;
  • mobile first principle;
  • adaptability for various devices;
  • graphics optimization and code minification.

I am constantly improving my professional skills. I am not afraid of difficult tasks and constantly experimenting.

My hobbies: neural networks, football, playing the guitar.

Hope for mutually beneficial cooperation.

Respectfully,
